Protecting Gretna For Over 89 Years

Gretna Fire & Rescue is a combination volunteer and career department serving the citizens of Gretna, Virginia and the surrounding Pittsylvania County communities. Built on a foundation of faith, integrity, and an unwavering commitment to training — our members stand ready around the clock.
