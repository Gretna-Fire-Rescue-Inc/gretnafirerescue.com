Who We Are

Protecting Gretna
For Over 89 Years

Gretna Fire & Rescue is a combination volunteer and career department serving the citizens of Gretna, Virginia and the surrounding Pittsylvania County communities.

We are built on a foundation of faith, integrity, and an unwavering commitment to training and professionalism. From structural firefighting to advanced emergency medical services, our members stand ready around the clock.

Whether you're a lifelong Gretna resident or new to our community — we're your neighbors, and we're always here when it matters most.

Structural, wildland, and vehicle fire response across District 22 and mutual aid coverage throughout Pittsylvania County.
